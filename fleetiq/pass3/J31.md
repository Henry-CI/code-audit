**Recommendations:**
1. Prioritize documenting the three HIGH severity findings first (getCustomers, saveasList, getPermision)
2. Add class-level Javadoc to all three classes explaining their purpose and relationships
3. Document all servlet doGet() methods with their supported parameters
4. Consider refactoring duplicated utility methods (mapValue, mapKey) into a shared utility class
